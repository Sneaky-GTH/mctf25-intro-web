If you visited http://172.12.0.6/tasktwo/buy?product=hat&price=5 directly, you would notice that doing so actually purchased the hat!

Let's take another look at the address - did you notice the question mark followed by words (often called "key-value pairs")? You can think of that as "data" that your browser is sending alongside your request. Think of it like this - you are sending a typical request to the server, but also telling the website that the "product" you're trying to buy is a "hat" (product=hat) and that the "price" is "5" (price=5).

Now think to yourself... what if instead of sending a request where the price was 5... what if it was 0? Or even better, a negative number?
